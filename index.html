The Rohlik Sales Forecasting Challenge on Kaggle tasks participants with predicting sales of online groceries. Using historical sales data, competitors create models to forecast demand, optimizing inventory, pricing, and promotions.

Rohlik Group, a leading European e-grocery innovator, operates across 11 warehouses in Czech Republic, Germany, Austria, Hungary, and Romania. This challenge focuses on predicting the sales of each selected warehouse inventory for the next 14 days.

Our final submission used a LightGBM-based model with features like temporal patterns, seasonal trends, and warehouse-specific behaviors. We also added a multiplier (1.05) to account for year-over-year sales growth.

We tried XGBoost and CatBoost, but LightGBM gave better results. We believe combining all three could have further improved our CV scores.
